About Jo Smith

I'm Jo Smith, the creative force behind the lens. With a background in Fine Arts and over a decade of experience in professional photography, I've honed my skills to create visually stunning and emotionally resonant images. Photography has always been more than a job to me; it's a way of connecting with the world and the people in it.
